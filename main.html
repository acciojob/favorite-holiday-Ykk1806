Lonavala


Lonavala is a beautiful hill station nestled in the Sahyadri ranges of Maharashtra, India. It is known for its lush greenery, misty mountains, waterfalls, and historic sites—making it a perfect getaway for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

  1. Tiger's Leap – A scenic cliff-top with panoramic valley views.
  2. Bhushi Dam – A popular spot especially during the monsoons for water fun.
  3. Karla Caves – Ancient Buddhist rock-cut caves with detailed carvings.
  4. Rajmachi Fort – Ideal for trekking and exploring historical architecture.
  5. Lonavala Lake – A peaceful place for picnics and nature walks.
  • Chikki – A sweet made from jaggery and peanuts, famous in Lonavala.
  • Vada Pav – A spicy potato fritter served in a bun, Maharashtra's favorite snack.
  • Kanda Bhaji – Crispy onion fritters perfect during rainy weather.
  • Misal Pav – A spicy curry served with bread, packed with flavors.
  • Masala Corn – Hot corn with spices, great for munching in the cool weather.
